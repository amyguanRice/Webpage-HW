Wind Speed

The wind speed vs latitude shows some correlation, despite the data scatter. Stronger winds are found in the southern hemisphere. This is because there is much smaller percentage of lands esp. mountains in the southern hemisphere, thus much less friction to reduce the wind energy, compared to the northern hemisphere.
