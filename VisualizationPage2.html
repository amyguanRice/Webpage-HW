Humidity

The relative humidity vs. latitude, shows a lot of scatter. There are two main reasons for the scatter: 1) the higher the air temperature, the higher water capacity of the air, while the definition of relative humidity is the ratio of actual humidity over the total capacity; even the warmer cities tend to have moist air, ratio is not necessarily higher due to the large water capacity; 2) the humidity is also highly dependent on the altitude and distance to the coasts, which are not accounted here.
